Power Yoga Class

Description

Power Yoga (based on Ashtanga sequence). This is intermediate level suitable for people with the knowledge of the basic yoga poses and who have a good fitness background. This class incorporates strong flowing sequence based on Ashtanga. Not suitable for people with serious health problems or after recent operations.
