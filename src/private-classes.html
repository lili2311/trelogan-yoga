Private Yoga classes

Description

Yoga for personal fitness. Gentle restorative yoga to suit your needs and aid with specific health problems. Partner yoga for couples is also available for couples wishing to work together on their fitness and improvements in their relationship through yoga using old yogic techniques. The classes can be tailored to your needs and abilities, please get in touch to find out more.

Private classes can also be arranged for a group of up to 5 people tailored to your needs and abilities.

The class is conducted online via Zoom so you can join from your living room! New to Zoom? To avoid a last-minute rush, set Zoom up on your phone or computer as soon as you have the class link. Simply click the link and it’ll prompt you to download the app. When it’s time to join the class, click the link again. Zoom has a useful help centre if you have any questions.
