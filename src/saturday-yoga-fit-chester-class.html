Pre-booking is required.

Description

Yoga-Fit is a Hatha-based Vinyasa style of yoga. It is a fitness-inspired yoga program designed to build strength and flexibility.

Yoga-Fit is yoga for everybody and every body, an athlete-friendly discipline offering options for poses of greater or lesser strengths and flows of movement.It encourages us to be in the moment, to let the poses fit our own bodies and to let go of judgement, expectations and competition.

We will be using yoga belts, foam rollers and a wall!
