Yoga for couples, partners, friends and singles in North Wales and Chester area.

Workshop Description:

Any shapes, level of flexibility and fitness are welcome! Previous yoga experience or fitness level doesn't matter.

We start with connecting and tapping into our energetic bodies, learn to be aware of energies of our loved ones, friends and people around us. I will lead you through the sequence of yoga poses suitable for total beginners and experienced athletes.We will use the bodies as props to work on flexibility, strength. Through breath and awareness learn how to connect to your partner. It is all about intimate relationships with yourself, loved ones, friends and people around you.

If you are single and wish to attend I will try to match you with another single person, no problem.

Have fun and treat you loved one, friend or just yourself to a unique opportunity to explore and experiment.
